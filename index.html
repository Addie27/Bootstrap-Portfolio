About Me

I am a business professional working toward a second career in software development. My dream is to build and deploy business applications that are practical, clean, user-friendly and allow employees to organize and access data efficiently. This passion comes from the many ways that software has helped and hindered me in my day-to-day work life.

When I'm not dreaming about ways to make work more organized, I am spending time with my dear husband and three beautiful children. I also enjoy reading, skiing, and golfing in my spare time.
